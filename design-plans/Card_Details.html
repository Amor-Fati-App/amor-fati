XVIII

Major Arcana Number 18 Pisces

The Moon

"The reflected light that deceives the eye, revealing the path through the subconscious mind."

Upright

Illusion Fear Anxiety Subconscious Intuition

The Moon represents a time of uncertainty and illusion, when nothing is as it seems. Be careful of making fast decisions, as you may realize later that you only had half the information. You need to listen to and trust your intuition so you can see past the illusion.

Reversed

Release of Fear Unhappiness Confusion Deception

Reversed, The Moon indicates that the illusions are fading, and you are beginning to see the truth of the situation. The fears that held you back are dissipating as clarity returns.

Symbolism & Imagery

A full moon (with a crescent within) hangs in the sky between two large towers. In the foreground, a small crayfish crawls out of a pool of water composed of the subconscious darker mind. A dog and a wolf stand in the grassy field, howling at the moon, representing the tamed and wild aspects of our minds.

The Crayfish

Emerging from the primordial waters, symbolizing the early stages of consciousness unfolding.

The Towers

Represent the dichotomy of good and evil, and the gateway into the unknown.

Dog & Wolf

The dual nature of the mind: the civilized, domestic self versus the feral, primal instinct.

The Path

A narrow winding road that leads toward the mountains, signifying the long journey to higher consciousness.
